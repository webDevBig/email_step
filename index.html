5 steps to improve the way you shop.

"this app has been made to make shopping easier and more efficient"

1 Step. Start with adding friends

what is the point of posting something if no one can see it? sometimes saving friends 20 bucks can feel like you gave them that 20. Invite friends and make shopping smarter

2nd step. post about your shopping experiences on got it list

you can add the referral link to your post to make it easy to shop for the same item. You can also share your post on other social media. Pretty easy! be helpful to your friends with your every review.

3rd step. Fill your wishlist

share your damn wishlist with friends! There are tons of benefits to doing it right now. 1st no one would ever give you the "wrong" present. 2nd you could store all your desired items in one place with links. 3rd invoke the power of visualization here

4th step. shop online with friends through screen sharing

it's like going to an online mall together. easy

5th STEP. make a poll among friends if you have hesitated

sometimes better solution is on the distance of just one question. Ask and help others to decide
